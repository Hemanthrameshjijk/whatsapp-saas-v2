# WhatsApp AI Store - Marketing & Growth Suite

This repository contains the frontend and backend for the WhatsApp AI SaaS platform, featuring advanced marketing automation and customer intelligence.

## 🚀 Recent Features

### 1. Marketing Intelligence
*   **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Automatically detect when customers leave items in their cart and send recovery nudges.
*   **Direct Broadcasts**: Send personalized broadcast messages to all customers or high-value VIP segments.
*   **Link Tracking**: Every link sent via WhatsApp is automatically wrapped in a tracking URL to measure **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** and engagement.

### 2. Influencer Partnership Manager
*   **Custom Tracking Links**: Generate measurement links for influencers (Instagram, YouTube, Bio links).
*   *Custom Short Codes**: Support for pretty links e.g. `ai.store/l/RAHUL20`.
*   **Product Deep-Links**: Links that open WhatsApp with a pre-filled product interest message: *"Hi! I'm interested in [Product] (Ref: Rahul)"*.

### 3. Customer Intelligence & Attribution
*   **Referral Tracking**: Customers are automatically tagged with their referral source (Influencer Name) on their first interaction.
*   **Acquisition Stats**: track exactly how many **new customers** each influencer has brought into the store.
*   **LTV & Stats**: Real-time view of customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and order history.

### 4. Human Handoff & Hybrid Chat
*   **Agent Takeover**: Manually override the AI with a single click to enter "Human Mode".
*   **Direct Messaging**: Reply directly to customers from the dashboard via the WhatsApp API.
*   **Mode Toggles**: Seamlessly switch between AI Assistant and Human Agent for any conversation.

## 🛠️ Configuration
*   **AI Settings**: Reorganized settings into **Store Profile**, **AI Intelligence**, and **Policies & FAQ**.
*   **Handoff Rules**: Configure when the AI should automatically flag a human agent (e.g., negative sentiment, medical queries).
